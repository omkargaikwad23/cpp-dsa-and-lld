I had the Googliness round a couple of days back, which focused mainly on behavioral questions. They asked about my hobbies outside work, how I’ve handled intra-team conflicts, and how I prioritize tasks when two tasks with the same priority come up. They also asked about past failures and what steps I took to prevent them from happening again. Another question was about an instance when I caught a teammate doing something unethical and how I handled it. Overall, the interview was more like a discussion where they were interested in hearing about my past experiences and how I’ve managed similar situations in previous roles. It went really well! The interviewer was friendly and engaging.
